What is DeScipline?

DeScipline is a revolutionary Web3 productivity tool that helps you maintain focus and build better habits through blockchain technology and smart contracts. By combining traditional productivity techniques with decentralized finance, we create a unique incentive system that helps you stay committed to your goals.

Key Features

  • Smart Contract Integration

    Set up productivity commitments backed by smart contracts on StarkNet

  • Focus Tracking

    Monitor and analyze your productivity sessions

  • Reward System

    Earn tokens for completing focused work sessions

  • Community Challenges

    Join group challenges and compete with others

How It Works

  1. Connect your StarkNet wallet
  2. Set up your productivity goals
  3. Start focused work sessions
  4. Earn rewards for completed sessions
  5. Build lasting productive habits
